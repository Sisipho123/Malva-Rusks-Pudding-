Malva Pudding Rusks Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/2 cup apricot jam
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 cups self-raising flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a large bowl, beat sugar and eggs until fluffy.
  3. Mix in milk, vinegar, apricot jam, and melted butter.
  4. Add flour and salt. Mix until a dough forms.
  5. Spread dough in a greased baking tin and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Let it cool completely. Cut into rusk-sized fingers.
  7. Place pieces on a wire rack and dry them out in the oven at 100°C (210°F) for 3–4 hours until crisp.
Enjoy your homemade Malva Pudding Rusks!
